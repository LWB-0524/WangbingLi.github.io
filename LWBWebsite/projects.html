Project 3: In-Situ Structural Construction Monitoring Using Mixed Reality and Ontology
May 2023 - Present

Industry Collaboration with Beijing Construction Engineering Group

Advisor

Assoc. Prof. Xuefeng Zhao | Project Managers: Liang Fu and Zhenqing Yang

Techniques

Midas, MySQL, IfeOpenShell, Protégé, Apache Jena, Unity, MRTK

Achievements
